No Yoke Chicken Pasta

Its a set it & forget it kind of recipe! Roughly 440 Calories, 43g protein, 6 fats, & 52g carbs. This recipe can make 7 meals!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken
  • 2 cans cream of chicken soup
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp thyme
  • 1 bag no yoke dumpling pasta
  • 1 bag frozen veggies

Instructions

  1. In a crockpot add chicken, cream of chicken soup, chicken broth, salt, pepper, garlic powder, & thyme. Give it a quick stir, cover, & let that cook on high for 4 hours.
  2. Uncover, shred the chicken, add pasta & frozen veggies. Cover again & let that cook for 45 minutes. I like to give it a nice stir around the 20-25 minute mark.
  3. Enjoy! For meal preppers disperse into 7 containers.
